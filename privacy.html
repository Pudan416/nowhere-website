Privacy Policy

Effective April 2026

Nowhere ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

Nowhere may process:

  • Health data you allow via HealthKit (such as step count and sleep data)
  • App usage/control data needed for blocking and unlock features (Family Controls / Device Activity)
  • Account-related data (if you create/sign in to an account)
  • Basic diagnostic/technical data needed to operate and improve the app

2. How We Use Data

We use data to:

  • Run core app features (rays balance, activity tracking, app access rules)
  • Sync your settings and account data (if applicable)
  • Maintain app security and reliability
  • Provide support and fix bugs

3. Health Data

Health data is used only for app functionality you enable.

We do not use Health data for advertising.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited data with service providers that help us operate the app (for example, backend infrastructure), under confidentiality and security obligations.

5. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

6. Your Choices

You can:

  • Revoke Health access in iOS Settings
  • Adjust notifications and permissions in iOS Settings
  • Request account/data deletion by contacting us

7. Children

Nowhere is not directed to children under 13.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

9. International Users

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, subject to applicable safeguards.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post updates on this page with a new effective date.

11. Contact

If you have privacy questions or requests, contact:

Email: hello@itsnowhere.net
