how it works

One loop. Three moves.

1 notice. walk, sleep, move, think, connect — your real-world activity fills a daily pool of 100 colors.

2 spend. use your colors to unlock feeds. pick a time window — 10 min, 30 min, an hour.

3 repeat. each day is a new canvas. colors reset, feeds close, and the loop starts fresh.
